Meet Muu!

This is Muu. Muu works at Mutsumi Hospital. Muu does not have a medical degree, but Muu helps people heal. Muu is very friendly and kind. Muu is as large as Muu is kind.

At Mutsumi Hospital, there is the concept of a road to recovery. It isn't straight or easy to navigate. That's why there are ムツミン guides, like Muu, who will make it easier to recover. Muu's goal is to help patients realize their dreams.

Muu is very sweet and helpful. Muu helps people recover, with a positive attitude. Other than that, we don't know what Muu is. Muu is decidedly mysterious, but Muu has very friendly energy. That is what matters. There are many guides on the road to recovery, but Muu has the biggest heart of them all.

Let's Learn About Muu!

Muu is very active. Muu likes to dance and play softball with friends.

Muu likes to go bird-watching. Muu's best friend is a bird.

Muu eats pudding every day. Muu also likes strawberries a lot.

About the Hospital

Mutsumi serves patients from the Tokushima area. Mutsumi offers both in-patient and out-patient care. In-patient stays are for a duration of 3 months. The hospital has many facilities, including restaurants and special programs.
